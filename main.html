About Us

Sparks Foundation is a non-profit organisation that benefits underprivileged children and their families through various projects in healthcare, education, nutrition, shelter, livelihood, women empowerment, child protection, and other social issues. It was founded under the Society Registration Act, 1860, and the Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950. The Sparks Foundation collaborates with society to bring about constructive change. Since its establishment, the organisation has sought to empower the most vulnerable members of society, with a focus on children and women, by allowing them to live a life of dignity and independence.
